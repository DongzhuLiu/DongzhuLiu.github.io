About me

I am currently a postdoctoral research associate at the Department of Engineering, King's College London, working with Prof. Osvaldo Simeone. I received the PhD degree from the University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Dr. Kaibin Huang in 2019, and the B.Eng. degree from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) in 2015.

Research Interests

  • Edge AI

  • Differential Privacy

  • Wireless Networks

  • Signal Processing

I am looking for PhD students who are self-motivated and have a strong background in machine learning, wireless communications, or signal processing. Feel free to contact me if you are interested.
